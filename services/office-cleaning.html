A Workspace That Works
as Hard as You Do.

Studies consistently show that a clean, well-maintained office increases productivity, reduces sick days, and creates a more positive atmosphere for everyone in the building. But the reality is, most businesses settle for "acceptable" when they could have exceptional.

Immaculate Guild's office cleaning service delivers a consistent, verifiable standard every single visit. Dedicated teams, quality checklists, and flexible scheduling ensure your office always makes the right impression — on your team and your clients.

Flexible Scheduling Options

Bespoke Schedule

What's Included

  • Workstation cleaning — desks, monitors, keyboards, phones
  • All floors — vacuumed, mopped, and entrance mats maintained
  • Meeting rooms cleaned and reset after every use
  • Kitchen and break room — full clean and hygiene maintained
  • Washrooms sanitised and consumables restocked
  • Reception and waiting areas presented to client-facing standard
  • Internal glass — partitions, doors, and windows
  • High-touch surfaces disinfected — door handles, light switches, lift buttons
  • All waste collected and bins relined throughout
