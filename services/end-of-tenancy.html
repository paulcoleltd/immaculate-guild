End of Tenancy Cleaning
Liverpool & Merseyside

Protect your deposit, satisfy your letting agent, and leave your property in flawless condition. Comprehensive, thorough, and guaranteed to meet any inventory check.

Get a Free Quote Call Us Now

Leave Nothing Behind.
Except a Spotless Property.

Moving out is stressful enough without worrying about the state of the property. Letting agents and landlords apply the highest possible standard to their checkout inspections — and a missed detail can cost you part or all of your deposit.

Our end of tenancy cleaning service is specifically designed to meet and exceed those standards. We clean to the exact level required for a successful inventory check-out, leaving the property in better condition than most tenants ever achieve. The result: your deposit back, on time, in full.

Deposit Protection Guarantee

If your letting agent or landlord raises a cleaning-related issue after our clean, we'll return and rectify it free of charge. We stand by our work completely.

What's Included

  • Full kitchen deep clean — inside oven, fridge, cupboards, extractor
  • All bathroom and en-suite surfaces, grout, and fixtures
  • All floors vacuumed, mopped, and spot-cleaned
  • Inside all cupboards, wardrobes, and drawers
  • Skirting boards, dado rails, and coving
  • Window frames, sills, and internal glass
  • Light switches, sockets, and door handles sanitised
  • Limescale removal from taps, shower heads, and tiles
  • Marks removed from walls (where possible)
  • Carpet steam cleaning available as an add-on
100% Eco-Friendly Products

We use environmentally friendly, non-toxic cleaning products throughout — effective enough to meet the most demanding inventory standards without harsh chemicals.
