What We Stand For

Three values sit at the core of everything we do. They're not marketing copy — they're the standard every clean is held to.

Trusted

Every cleaner is DBS-checked, reference-verified, and fully insured before they set foot in your property. We take the responsibility of access seriously — and we honour it every single visit.

Transparent

No hidden fees. No vague quotes. No day-of surprises. We give you a clear price upfront, we explain exactly what's included, and we honour that commitment — every time.

Tailored

No two properties are the same. We listen, we assess, and we build a clean around your specific needs — not a one-size-fits-all package designed for the average customer.

Eco-Conscious

Every product we use is eco-friendly, non-toxic, and safe for children, pets, and the environment. We refuse to compromise on results, but we won't do it at the planet's expense.

Consistent

The same high standard, every visit. We assign dedicated cleaners to recurring clients so you always know who's coming — and they know exactly how you like things done.

Guaranteed

Not satisfied? We come back and put it right — no questions, no charge. Our 100% satisfaction guarantee isn't a policy statement, it's a promise we keep.

What You Can Always Expect

We've built Immaculate Guild around the things that actually matter to clients — not the things that look good in a brochure. Here's what every client gets, every time, without having to ask:

  • A confirmed arrival window — no vague "between 8am and 6pm" windows
  • The same cleaner each visit for recurring services
  • Eco-friendly, non-toxic products on every clean
  • A 3-hour response to all enquiries
  • An honest quote before any work begins
  • A satisfaction guarantee on every service
  • Respectful, professional conduct in your space at all times
