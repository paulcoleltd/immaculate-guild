3. Pricing & Payment

All prices are quoted in pounds sterling and include VAT where applicable. Payment is due as follows:

  • We accept bank transfer, card payment, and other methods confirmed at booking

4. Cancellations & Rescheduling

We understand plans change. Our cancellation policy is:

  • More than 48 hours' notice: Free cancellation or reschedule

  • Less than 24 hours' notice: 100% of the booked service fee applies
  • No access on the day: Full fee charged plus any reasonable travel costs

We reserve the right to cancel or reschedule in exceptional circumstances (staff illness, extreme weather, emergencies). We will give you as much notice as possible and offer the earliest available alternative date.

  • You are responsible for securing or removing valuables, fragile items and irreplaceable objects prior to our visit

  • Any known hazards (asbestos, mould, pest activity) must be disclosed before booking

  • Maintaining full public liability insurance for all services
